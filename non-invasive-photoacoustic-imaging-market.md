<p><p>Non-invasive Photoacoustic Imaging (PAI) is an advanced imaging technique that combines ultrasound and laser-induced light to visualize tissue morphology and function. By using laser pulses, this method generates ultrasound signals from tissues, which are then processed to form high-resolution images.
