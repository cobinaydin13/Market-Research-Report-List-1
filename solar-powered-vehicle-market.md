<p><p>The solar-powered vehicle market comprises primarily two types: Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) and Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs). BEVs rely exclusively on electric power stored in batteries, often supplemented by solar panels for recharging, making them environmentally friendly and efficient. In contrast, HEVs combine an internal combustion engine with an electric motor, utilizing solar energy to enhance overall efficiency while reducing fuel consumption. Both types contribute to sustainability by decreasing dependence on fossil fuels and minimizing greenhouse gas emissions.</p></p>
