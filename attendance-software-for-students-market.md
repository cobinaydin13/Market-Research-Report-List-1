<p><p>Attendance software for students is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions allow schools to access attendance data online via the internet, offering flexibility and ease of use, with automatic updates and reduced IT maintenance. Conversely, on-premises software is installed locally on school servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring more resource allocation for maintenance and infrastructure. Both options serve to streamline attendance tracking and enhance administrative efficiency.</p></p>
