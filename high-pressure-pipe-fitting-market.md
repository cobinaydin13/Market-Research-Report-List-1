<p><p>The high-pressure pipe fitting market encompasses various types that facilitate fluid flow in industrial applications. Straight fittings allow for direct connections between pipes, ensuring smooth transitions. Elbow fittings change the direction of flow, typically at 90 or 45 degrees. Three-way fittings enable branching of flow, while four-way fittings allow for connections at multiple access points. Additionally, "others" include specialized fittings, such as reducers and caps, which cater to unique piping requirements, enhancing system efficiency and functionality.</p></p>
