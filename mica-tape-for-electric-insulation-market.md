<p><p>Mica tape for electric insulation includes two primary types: mica glass tape and mica polyester tape. Mica glass tape features a reinforcement of glass fibers, enhancing its mechanical strength and thermal resistance, making it suitable for high-temperature applications. In contrast, mica polyester tape utilizes a polyester film backing, offering flexibility and a degree of insulation while being lighter and easier to handle. Both types are essential in various electrical applications, ensuring reliable insulation and protection against heat and electrical currents.</p></p>
