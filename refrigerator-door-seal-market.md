<p><p>The refrigerator door seal market comprises various materials: PVC, TPE, TPU, and others. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is widely used for its flexibility and cost-effectiveness. TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) offers excellent resilience and weather resistance, making it suitable for energy-efficient seals. TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) provides durability and good chemical resistance, enhancing seal longevity. These materials ensure effective insulation and energy efficiency in refrigerator designs.</p></p>
