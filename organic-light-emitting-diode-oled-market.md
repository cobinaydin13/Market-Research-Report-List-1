<p><p>The Organic Light-Emitting Diode (OLED) market can be categorized into three types: monochrome, multi-color, and full-color. Monochrome OLEDs display a single color and are often used in simple applications like indicators and clocks. Multi-color OLEDs combine multiple colors to produce a broader range of hues, suitable for devices like smartphones and wearable tech. Full-color OLEDs represent the most advanced type, capable of displaying vibrant images and videos, making them ideal for televisions, monitors, and high-end displays.</p></p>
