<p><p>The CCD cooled camera market primarily comprises area scan and line scan cameras, each serving distinct imaging needs. Area scan cameras capture full-frame images simultaneously, ideal for applications requiring detailed capture of static scenes, such as inspection or documentation. In contrast, line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, making them suitable for continuous processes like conveyor systems or print inspection. Both types leverage cooling technology to enhance image quality by reducing thermal noise during critical imaging tasks.</p></p>
