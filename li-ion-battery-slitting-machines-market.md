<p><p>Li-ion battery slitting machines are categorized into three types based on operation: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require operator intervention for each step, offering lower initial costs but less efficiency. Semi-automatic machines streamline the process, combining manual input with automated features to enhance productivity and precision. Fully automatic machines operate independently, providing high-speed performance and minimal human oversight, ideal for large-scale production. Each type caters to different manufacturing needs and budget considerations within the industry.</p></p>
