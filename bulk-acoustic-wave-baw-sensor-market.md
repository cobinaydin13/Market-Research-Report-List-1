<p><ul><li>Resonators BAW</li><li>Delay Lines BAW</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) sensor market consists of two main types: resonators and delay lines. Resonator BAW sensors utilize piezoelectric materials to generate resonant frequencies, making them ideal for applications requiring precise frequency control, such as telecommunications and filtering. Delay line BAW sensors, on the other hand, operate by measuring the time delay of acoustic waves traveling through a medium, which is useful for sensing changes in pressure, temperature, or other environmental variables. Both types leverage acoustic waves for enhanced sensing capabilities.</p></p>
