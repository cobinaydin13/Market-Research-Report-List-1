<p><p>The Rear Occupant Alert Systems market is segmented into two main types: pressure sensors and ultrasonic sensors. Pressure sensors detect weight on the rear seats, alerting drivers if a passenger is present, enhancing safety during exits. Ultrasonic sensors utilize sound waves to monitor the rear space, identifying movement or the presence of objects, ensuring no one is left behind. Both technologies aim to prevent accidents and increase awareness of rear seat occupants, promoting safety for children and pets.</p></p>
