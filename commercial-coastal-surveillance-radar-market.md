<p><p>The commercial coastal surveillance radar market primarily features two radar types: X-Band and S-Band radar. X-Band radar operates at higher frequencies, providing detailed imagery and precise detection, making it ideal for small target identification in coastal areas. In contrast, S-Band radar operates at lower frequencies, offering greater range and effectiveness in adverse weather conditions. Both radar types are essential for monitoring maritime activities, enhancing coastal security, and facilitating search and rescue operations, each serving specific operational needs within coastal surveillance systems.</p></p>
