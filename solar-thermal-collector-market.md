<p><p>The solar thermal collector market comprises various types, each serving distinct applications. Flat plate collectors are widely used for residential water heating due to their simple design and cost-effectiveness. Evacuated tube collectors offer higher efficiency for both heating and industrial applications, as they minimize heat loss. Solar air collectors are designed to heat air for space heating or ventilation purposes.
