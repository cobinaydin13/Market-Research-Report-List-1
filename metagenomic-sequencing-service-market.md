<p><strong>The Metagenomic Sequencing Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Metagenomic Sequencing Service Market encompasses various techniques for analyzing microbial communities. Shotgun metagenomic sequencing provides a comprehensive view by sequencing all DNA fragments, revealing diverse organisms. In contrast, 16S rRNA sequencing targets specific gene regions to identify and classify bacteria. Metatranscriptomics focuses on RNA to understand active gene expression within communities. Whole-genome sequencing involves sequencing entire genomes for detailed insights, while de novo assembly reconstructs genomes from scratch, facilitating the study of novel organisms without prior reference sequences.</p></p>
