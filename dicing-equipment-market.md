<p><ul><li>Grinding Wheel Dicing Machine</li><li>Laser Dicing Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dicing equipment market includes two primary types: grinding wheel dicing machines and laser dicing machines. Grinding wheel dicing machines utilize abrasive grinding wheels to slice through materials, offering high precision and efficiency for hard substrates. In contrast, laser dicing machines employ focused laser beams to cut materials, providing flexibility and minimal material damage, ideal for delicate or complex layouts. Both types are essential in semiconductor and electronics manufacturing, catering to diverse production needs and material properties.</p></p>
