<p><ul><li>Integral Steering Axle</li><li>Split Steering Axle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steering axle market comprises two primary types: integral steering axles and split steering axles. Integral steering axles are designed as a single unit, integrating both steering and axle functions, offering simplicity and reduced weight, making them ideal for smaller vehicles. Conversely, split steering axles consist of separate components for steering and axle functions, providing greater flexibility and customization for larger vehicles. Both types serve distinct applications, influenced by vehicle size, weight distribution, and maneuverability requirements.</p></p>
