<p><p>The EV in-wheel motor market comprises two main types: brush and brushless motors. Brush motors utilize mechanical brushes for electrical connection, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, but with higher maintenance and wear concerns. In contrast, brushless motors use electronic controllers for improved efficiency, durability, and performance, making them suitable for higher-end EV applications. As electric vehicle technology advances, brushless motors are gaining traction for their advantages in enhancing range, power, and overall vehicle dynamics, while brush motors still find relevance in budget-friendly options.</p></p>
