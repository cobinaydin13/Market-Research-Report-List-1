<p><strong>The Optical Absolute Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Singleturn Encoder</li><li>Multiturn Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical absolute encoders market is divided into two main types: single-turn and multi-turn encoders. Single-turn encoders provide absolute position data within one rotation, making them ideal for applications with limited rotational range. In contrast, multi-turn encoders track position across multiple rotations, utilizing additional mechanisms to retain positional information even when power is lost. Both types are crucial in automation, robotics, and industrial applications, offering precise positioning and improved control systems for various machinery and equipment.</p></p>
