<p><p>The ozone technology market is segmented into several methods for ozone generation. The electrolysis method uses electrical energy to split water molecules, producing ozone efficiently. The nuclear radiation method utilizes radioactive sources to generate ozone by ionizing air, offering high concentrations. The ultraviolet method employs UV light to interact with oxygen, creating ozone without chemical byproducts. Additionally, other methods include spark discharge and corona discharge techniques. These variations cater to diverse industrial applications, enhancing ozone's role in water treatment and air purification.</p></p>
