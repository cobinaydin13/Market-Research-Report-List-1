<p><ul><li>Single Piston</li><li>Double Piston</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Continuous Screen Changers market is primarily divided into two types: Single Piston and Double Piston systems. Single Piston screen changers utilize one piston to filter out impurities in the polymer melt, providing efficient operation for lower viscosity materials. In contrast, Double Piston screen changers feature two pistons that allow for uninterrupted material flow during screen changes, enhancing productivity and reducing downtime. Both types are essential in various industries, optimizing the quality of extruded materials while ensuring process efficiency.</p></p>
