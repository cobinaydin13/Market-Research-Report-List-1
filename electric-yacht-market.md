<p><p>The electric yacht market is primarily divided into two types: pure electric and hybrid power yachts. Pure electric yachts operate exclusively on electric propulsion, utilizing battery systems that provide a sustainable and eco-friendly alternative to traditional fuel sources, ideal for short-range cruising. In contrast, hybrid power yachts combine both electric and traditional internal combustion engines, allowing for greater flexibility in range and fuel efficiency, catering to longer journeys while still reducing emissions. Both types reflect a growing trend towards greener boating.</p></p>
