<p><p>The multi-stage roots vacuum pump market comprises two main types: dry pumps and wet pumps. Dry pumps operate without lubricants or fluids, making them suitable for applications requiring high purity and reduced contamination, often utilized in semiconductor and pharmaceutical industries. In contrast, wet pumps use liquid lubricants to create a seal, providing efficient pumping for processes involving gases at lower vacuum levels. Both types cater to diverse sectors, allowing for tailored solutions in industrial and scientific applications.</p></p>
