<p><strong>The Cell Therapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stem Cell</li><li>Non-Stem Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cell therapy market can be categorized into two main types: stem cell therapy and non-stem cell therapy. Stem cell therapy utilizes undifferentiated cells that can develop into various cell types, enabling regeneration and treatment of conditions like blood disorders and neurodegenerative diseases. Non-stem cell therapy, on the other hand, involves differentiated cells that are already specialized, often used in treatments for conditions such as cartilage injuries or certain cancers. Both approaches aim to repair, replace, or enhance cellular functions for therapeutic benefits.</p></p>
