<p><p>The industrial ventilation equipment market includes various types of fans designed to improve airflow and maintain air quality in industrial settings. Centrifugal fans utilize a rotating impeller to draw air in and expel it at high pressure, making them suitable for applications requiring high resistance. Axial fans move air parallel to the fan's axis, providing efficient ventilation for spaces with lower pressure requirements. Other equipment may include exhaust hoods, air ducts, and filters, contributing to effective industrial ventilation systems.</p></p>
