CCTV plumbing cameras are versatile, typically used for inspecting residential or commercial drainage systems. They provide real-time video feedback, helping identify blockages and structural issues. Sewer crawler cameras, on the other hand, are designed for more challenging environments, capable of navigating through complex sewer systems and performing in-depth inspections. Both types enhance diagnostic accuracy and facilitate efficient plumbing maintenance and repair solutions.</p></p>
