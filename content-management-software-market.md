<p><p>Content Management Software (CMS) is categorized into two primary types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises CMS requires installation on local servers, offering greater control and customization, making it suitable for organizations with specific compliance needs. In contrast, cloud-based CMS operates on remote servers, providing scalability, automatic updates, and lower upfront costs, making it more accessible for businesses with limited IT resources. Each type serves diverse operational requirements and budgets, shaping the way organizations manage and distribute their digital content.</p></p>
