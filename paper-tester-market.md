<p><p>The paper tester market is classified into two primary types: physical testers and optical testers. Physical testers assess various mechanical properties of paper, such as tensile strength, thickness, and tear resistance, ensuring that the paper meets specific functional requirements. Optical testers, on the other hand, evaluate the visual and optical characteristics of paper, including brightness, color, and opacity. Both types of testers are essential for quality control in the paper industry, ensuring products perform as intended in their applications.</p></p>
