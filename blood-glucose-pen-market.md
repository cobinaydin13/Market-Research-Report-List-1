<p><p>The blood glucose pen market primarily consists of Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) and Blood Glucose Monitoring (BGM) systems. CGM devices provide real-time glucose level readings through sensors placed under the skin, offering continuous data for better management of diabetes. In contrast, BGM systems require users to manually prick their finger to obtain blood samples for intermittent glucose readings. Both types serve critical roles in diabetes management, catering to different user preferences and clinical needs for monitoring blood sugar levels.</p></p>
