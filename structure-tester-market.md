<p><p>The Structure Tester market is categorized into two main types: benchtop structure testers and floor structure testers. Benchtop structure testers are compact, portable devices designed for testing smaller samples in laboratory settings, providing precise measurements for material properties. In contrast, floor structure testers are larger and more robust, used for on-site evaluations of significant structural components like beams and slabs. Both types are essential for assessing structural integrity and ensuring safety in construction and engineering applications.</p></p>
