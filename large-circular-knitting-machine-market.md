<p><strong>The Large Circular Knitting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Circular Knitting Machines</li><li>Double Circular Knitting Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The large circular knitting machine market comprises two main types: single circular knitting machines and double circular knitting machines. Single circular machines operate on a single needle bed, producing seamless tubular fabrics, ideal for items like hosiery and t-shirts. In contrast, double circular machines feature two needle beds, allowing for the creation of more complex patterns and thicker fabrics, suitable for production of sweaters and heavier textiles. Both types are essential for efficient, high-volume garment manufacturing.</p></p>
