<p><p>The medical sterile gloves market consists of various types, including latex, vinyl, nitrile rubber, and neoprene. Latex gloves offer flexibility and strong barrier protection but can cause allergic reactions in some users. Vinyl gloves are cost-effective alternatives but provide less durability and protection. Nitrile rubber gloves are resistant to punctures and chemicals, making them suitable for various medical applications, while neoprene gloves offer enhanced flexibility and chemical resistance, ideal for high-risk settings. Each type caters to specific needs in healthcare settings.</p></p>
