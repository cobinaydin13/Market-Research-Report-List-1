<p><p>The Reciprocating Gait Orthosis (RGO) market consists of three main types: Horizontal Cable RGO, Rocker Bar RGO, and Isocentric RGO. Horizontal Cable RGO utilizes cables to connect the orthosis, promoting natural gait movement. Rocker Bar RGO incorporates a rocking mechanism for smooth transitions between steps, enhancing stability. Isocentric RGO features a pivot point that allows for reciprocal motion in a controlled manner, optimizing gait efficiency. Each type is designed to meet specific rehabilitation needs for individuals with mobility impairments.</p></p>
