<p><strong>The Level Gauge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflex Level Gauge</li><li>Transparent Level Gauge</li><li>Magnetic Level Gauge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The level gauge market consists of various types designed for fluid measurement in industrial applications. Reflex level gauges use a prism design to allow visibility of the liquid level, displaying clear contrasts between the liquid and vapor. Transparent level gauges provide direct visibility through a glass tube, offering a simple and straightforward measurement. Magnetic level gauges utilize a magnetic float that rises and falls with fluid levels, providing a visual indication through an external indicator. These types cater to diverse needs in safety, accuracy, and visibility.</p></p>
