The market features various types: centrifugal separators utilize rotational movement to enhance separation efficiency; reciprocating separators use back-and-forth motion to facilitate material sorting; rotary separators employ a continuous rotating mechanism for ongoing separation processes; while diaphragm separators use a flexible barrier to help differentiate materials.
