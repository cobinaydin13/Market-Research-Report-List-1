<p><p>The in-vehicle operational amplifier (op-amp) market is segmented into single-channel, dual-channel, and quad-channel configurations. Quad-channel op-amps integrate four channels in one package, maximizing space and efficiency for complex systems like advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and infotainment. Each type addresses specific performance requirements and space constraints in automotive applications, enhancing functionality and reliability.</p></p>
