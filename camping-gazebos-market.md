<p><p>The camping gazebos market comprises two main types: pop-up gazebos and built-up gazebos. Pop-up gazebos are lightweight, easy to set up, and portable, ideal for casual users and short-term outdoor events. They typically feature a collapsible frame and waterproof fabric for quick assembly. Built-up gazebos, on the other hand, offer sturdier construction and more permanent solutions, often designed for long-term use in gardens or parks. These gazebos may include additional features such as privacy screens and enhanced durability for varying weather conditions.</p></p>
