<p><strong>The Nuclear Power Reactor Decommissioning Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressurized Water Reactor Decommissioning</li><li>Boiling Water Reactor Decommissioning</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nuclear power reactor decommissioning market is categorized primarily into three types: Pressurized Water Reactor (PWR) decommissioning, Boiling Water Reactor (BWR) decommissioning, and Others. PWR decommissioning involves the shutdown and dismantling of reactors using pressurized water as a coolant, often requiring complex procedures due to radiation concerns. BWR decommissioning entails similar processes but involves boiling water reactors where coolant boils in the reactor core. "Others" encompasses various non-standard reactor types and small modular reactors, each with unique decommissioning challenges.</p></p>
