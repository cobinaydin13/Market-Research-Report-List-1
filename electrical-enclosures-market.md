<p><p>The electrical enclosures market is categorized into three main types: wall-mounted, floor-mounted/free-standing, and underground enclosures. Wall-mounted enclosures are ideal for saving space in smaller areas and are commonly used in residential and commercial settings. Floor-mounted or free-standing enclosures offer versatility and robustness for housing larger electrical equipment in industrial environments. Underground enclosures are designed for protection against environmental factors, serving as secure housing for electrical components buried below ground, ensuring safety and reliability in utility applications.</p></p>
