<p><p>The automotive rubber hoses market is divided into three primary types: connect the hose, low-pressure hose, and high-pressure hose. Connect the hoses are designed for fluid transfer in various automotive systems, providing flexible connections. Low-pressure hoses are used in applications with minimal pressure requirements, ensuring fluid delivery without risk of rupture. In contrast, high-pressure hoses are engineered to withstand significant pressure, making them ideal for systems like fuel and hydraulic lines that require robust performance under demanding conditions.</p></p>
