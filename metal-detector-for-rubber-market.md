<p><p>The metal detector for rubber market includes two main types: conveyor type metal detectors and tunnel type metal detectors. Conveyor type metal detectors are integrated into production lines, scanning products as they move on conveyor belts, ensuring that metal contaminants are detected and removed efficiently. In contrast, tunnel type metal detectors create a dedicated scanning area where larger items or bulk materials pass through, allowing for thorough inspection. Both types enhance product safety and quality in the rubber manufacturing process.</p></p>
