<p><strong>The Radio Frequency Identification Transponder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive</li><li>Active</li><li>Semi Active</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) transponder market consists of three main types: passive, active, and semi-active. </p><p>Passive transponders lack their own power source and rely on energy from the RFID reader for operation, making them cost-effective for applications like inventory tracking. Active transponders have their own power source, allowing for longer read ranges and functionalities, suitable for real-time tracking. Semi-active transponders combine features of both, using a battery for processing while relying on the reader for communication, ideal for applications needing enhanced performance.</p></p>
