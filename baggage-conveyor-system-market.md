<p><p>The baggage conveyor system market includes various types designed for efficient baggage handling. The Delivery Conveyor transports luggage to the final destination, while the Injection Conveyor facilitates seamless transfer from one belt to another. The Queue Conveyor manages luggage flow in busy areas to prevent bottlenecks, and the Extended Queue Conveyor offers additional space for managing oversized or excess baggage. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing airport operations and enhancing passenger experience.</p></p>
