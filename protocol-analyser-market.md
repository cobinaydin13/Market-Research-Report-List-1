<p><p>The Protocol Analyser market comprises various types of instruments used for analyzing and testing communication protocols in electronic devices. Vector Network Analyzers (VNAs) measure complex electrical networks and provide both magnitude and phase information of S-parameters, essential for RF and microwave applications. In contrast, Scalar Network Analyzers (SNAs) focus on measuring the magnitude of S-parameters only, making them suitable for simpler tasks. Together, they facilitate advancements in telecommunications, aerospace, and consumer electronics by ensuring reliable and efficient network performance.</p></p>
