<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES)</li><li>Remote Keyless Entry System (RKES)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Keyless Vehicle Access Control Systems market comprises two primary types: Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES). PKES allows drivers to unlock and start vehicles without manual interaction, utilizing proximity sensors to detect key fobs. In contrast, RKES requires the user to press buttons on a remote control to lock or unlock the vehicle. Both systems enhance convenience and security, catering to the evolving demands of modern automotive technology.</p></p>
