<p><p>The automotive aroma diffuser market offers three primary types: liquid, solid, and aerosol. Liquid type diffusers use fragrant oils to create a pleasant atmosphere inside vehicles, often utilizing ultrasonic or evaporative methods. Solid type diffusers typically employ scented pads or gels that gradually release fragrance over time, making them easy to use and replace. Aerosol type diffusers dispense fragrance in a fine mist, providing immediate scent enhancement and often featuring pressurized cans for convenient application. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>
