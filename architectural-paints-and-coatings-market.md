<p><strong>The Architectural Paints and Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-Borne</li><li>Solvent-Borne</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The architectural paints and coatings market is categorized into three main types: water-borne, solvent-borne, and others. Water-borne paints are environmentally friendly, using water as a solvent, offering low VOC emissions and easy cleanup. Solvent-borne paints contain organic solvents, providing durability and better adhesion, but typically emit higher levels of VOCs. Other types may include powder coatings, oil-based paints, and specialty coatings, catering to specific applications and performance requirements, such as resistance to moisture or chemicals.</p></p>
